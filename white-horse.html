Brickasso® Registry: #01

The White Horse | Brickasso® No. 01

The White Horse is Brickasso® No. 01, the first official work in the Brickasso® body of work and the origin point of the practice. Built from 18,322 original LEGO® elements, it establishes the visual and material language that would define everything that followed.

The composition is driven by clarity, restraint and presence. Set against a Novacolor ARCHI+ Big background and held within a solid wood frame, the work balances sculptural surface with the contained energy of the figure. From a distance, the horse reads with calm force. At close range, the image opens into thousands of deliberate placements that structure light, movement and form.

The result is a foundational work, where precision and emotion meet without excess. The White Horse marks the beginning of Brickasso® not only as a signature, but as a method and identity. The piece is recorded as BRK-01 in the Brickasso® Registry and is accompanied by a signed and numbered Certificate of Authenticity.
