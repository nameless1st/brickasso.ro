About the Artist

Albert Oprea is the artist behind Brickasso®, a practice focused on one-of-one sculptural works built from LEGO® elements. Each piece is constructed entirely by hand, brick by brick, without the use of automated or generative software.

His work transforms thousands of individual elements into cohesive visual compositions, where structure, texture and narrative coexist. Every artwork is unique, signed, catalogued and accompanied by a private certificate of authenticity.

Albert began building with LEGO® at the age of four. Over time, loose bricks replaced instructions, and construction became creation. What started as play evolved into a personal visual language.

His early works explore iconic subjects, from watches to portraits, while developing a distinctive approach based on precision, patience and layered detail. Each piece reveals additional scenes and structures when viewed up close.

The White Horse marks a defining moment in his journey. It is the first official work, signed as number 01, and the foundation of the Brickasso® identity.

Today, Brickasso® represents an ongoing exploration of scale, complexity and expression, where each new work builds on the last.
