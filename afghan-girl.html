Brickasso® Registry: #03

Afghan Girl | Brickasso® No. 03

Afghan Girl is Brickasso® No. 03, a licensed one-of-one work developed as a study of presence, memory and human dignity. The portrait does not rely on scale alone. It holds its force through gaze, tension and the concentration of emotion within a fixed image.

Built by hand from original LEGO® elements and based on the licensed photograph by Steve McCurry, the work translates one of the most recognized photographic images into material structure. The surface moves between fragility and resistance, where color, density and placement carry the emotional weight of the portrait.

Presented on Românii au Talent, Romania, 2026.
